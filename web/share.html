Share/Unshare Snova(GAE) AppID

Note:

  • You can share your snova appid to people who do NOT known how to deploy snova(GAE) server.
  • An email account needed as a verify id for sharing/unsharing appid.
  • The shared appid can be used by anybody as 'anonymouse' user.
  • You can use snova admin tool to control traffic and blacklist for 'anonymouse'.
